At Hand to Heart, we are more than an NGO; we are a collective of compassionate individuals dedicated to making a difference. Founded on the principle of extending a helping hand with genuine care, Hand to Heart is committed to addressing pressing global issues. We believe that every act of kindness has the power to change the world, and we channel our energy into humanitarian efforts, community development, and social empowerment. Our journey is driven by the belief that together, we can create a brighter, more equitable future for all. Explore our initiatives, get involved, and join us in shaping a world where hearts and hands work in harmony to bring about positive change.
To extend our compassionate hands to the world, providing vital support and care where it's needed most. We are dedicated to alleviating suffering, promoting education, and empowering communities. Our mission is driven by a simple belief: that each act of kindness can create a ripple effect of positive change, ultimately leading to a better, more equitable world.
Our vision is a world where every heart is touched by empathy and every hand is willing to help. We envision communities thriving with access to quality education, healthcare, and essential resources. We aspire to be a driving force behind a global movement, where compassion, cooperation, and innovation unite to solve the most pressing challenges of our time. Hand to Heart's vision is a brighter, more inclusive future, achieved one heartfelt gesture at a time

What We Do?

We believe that we can save more lives with you

Healthy Food

We're passionate about good nutrition. Hand to Heart ensures everyone has access to fresh, balanced meals for a healthier life.

Pure Water

Clean water is a basic right. We work to provide communities with safe, uncontaminated water sources, promoting health and well-being

Social Care

Hand to Heart cares for the vulnerable. We support the elderly, children, and those in need, offering hope and dignity to our communities.
